## Context

### Course

Introduction to Computer Science - 9/10th grade 

### Situation
This is an imaginary class based on the idea that I would continue teaching in the same district I am currently in but at the high school level. I am going to use as the constraints, the constraints at my current school in order to be able to work off of what I know. 

All students will have chromebooks assigned by the school. Some students will not have their chromebooks because they are lost and/or broken and in the process of being replaced. I will have access to a cart of chromebooks for students to use. Budget will be low so I will want to get as much out of these chromebooks as possible through the use of mostly cloud applications/programming. This will limit hardware used in the class.
